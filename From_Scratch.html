Snoopy And His Alter-Egos

World War 1 Flying Ace


  1. His worst enemy is the Red Baron
    • He gets shot at often by the Red Baron
  2. He flys the "Sopwith Camel" airplane model.
  3. His missions are usually to the French countryside.

Joe Cool


  1. He is the coolest guy you will ever meet.
    • Like seriously, he is SO cool.
  2. He has some sick dance moves.
  3. He knows just how to make the ladies swoon.

World Famous Author


  1. He uses a typewriter to write his novels.
  2. Many of his stories begin during a "Dark and Stormy Night"
  3. He awaits the day for someone to publish his novels.
